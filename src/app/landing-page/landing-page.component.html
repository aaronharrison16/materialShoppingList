Material Shopping List Simplify your Shopping

This is a simple shopping list application created with the intent of keeping your shopping trips painless and organized.

To use the application all you need is a Gmail account. From there, you simply add your items to your list, and as you add the items to your cart, add them to your cart in the app as well.

Simple as that.
